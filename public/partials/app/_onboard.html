Welcome to Field Guide.

We'll help you run user research quickly and easily.

This is a Report. It has all of the Learnings and Next Steps you found from speaking to Participants. This is put together after conducting your research!

A Report is a simple way to help your team understand what you learned from research, and what actions to take in order to improve your product.

The way you get a great Report is by taking great notes during research.

Your notes are sorted according to the Topic you currently have selected. This automatically sorts them for you in your Report.

Add #Tags to your notes to highlight important information. We'll give you some suggestions on valuable types of feedback to lookout for.

When you end a Session write some quick summary notes of the insights and issues you identified. These notes will help you create an overall project report much quicker.

If you want to see what's happening on your Participant's screen during a Session, you will need to download the Field Guide iOS app on your testing device or get the Participant to download the app. Get it now from the iTunes Store
